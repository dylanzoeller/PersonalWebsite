My Interests & Portfolio

Music

Music is probably the interest that impacts my day-to-day life the most. There's almost never a time when I don't have something playing, whether I'm driving, working out, walking around the city, or just relaxing at home.

One of my favorite things about music is how every artist has their own story and style. I enjoy listening to a variety of genres because different types of music fit different moods and situations. Some days call for something upbeat and energetic, while others are better with something more laid-back.

I also love live music because it creates experiences that you can't get from just listening through headphones. Austin's music scene is one of the reasons I was excited to move here, and I'm always looking for new venues, artists, and events to check out.

Fashion

Fashion has always interested me because it's one of the easiest ways to express your personality without saying a word.

I enjoy seeing how different people put outfits together and use clothing to communicate confidence, creativity, and individuality. My personal style has changed a lot over the years, but I've learned that the best outfits are usually the ones that feel authentic rather than trendy.

What I like most about fashion is that it's always evolving. New brands, new ideas, and new influences constantly keep things interesting. It's fun to experiment with different looks and find inspiration from music, culture, sports, and everyday life.

Boxing

Boxing is something I admire because it combines physical ability with mental toughness.

At first glance, most people focus on the physical side of the sport, but what really interests me is the discipline behind it. Success in boxing comes from consistency, preparation, and the willingness to keep improving even when progress feels slow.

The sport teaches valuable lessons that apply outside the gym as well. Things like patience, resilience, confidence, and accountability are all important both in boxing and in everyday life.
